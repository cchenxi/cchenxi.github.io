    sentinel0.conf

    sentinel monitor mymaster 127.0.0.1 6379 2

    4.1. 准备配置文件#

    port 6379
cluster-enabled yes
cluster-config-file "node-6379.conf"

dbfilename "dump-6379.rdb"
daemonize yes
